How to Play:

Move Tiles
Click on a letter tile to select/de-select it.
Click on an empty square to move the selected tile there.
Tiles can only move to a square if there is an unobstructed path to reach it.

Score Words
Make words reading left to right or top to bottom, like Scrabble.
Valid words are highlighted automatically
Click a highlighted tile to score all connected highlighted tiles.
Points = size of entire group, squared.
Make multiple words touching or intersecting and score them as one big group.
